Porter Williams

I am a software engineering major. I love to do anything I haven't done yet, especially if that involves the outdoors. I play guitar and sing and write songs for my wife, but you're more likely to find us watching Psych or Boy Meets World or competing with eachother on 'the Office' mobile game events. We don't plan on having kids until I've got a good programing job so that she can stay home with them. That's why I'm doing this, so that I can make a good, relaxed life for my wife and future kids. I have always been a member. I recently saw a quote by Elder Cook in, 'The Doctrine of the Father' that says, "Even for those who...have lived unrighteously but have not rebelled against God, a loving Father has prepared kingdoms of glory that are superior to our existence here on earth. There can be no doubt of the Father's love for His spirit children." I like this quote because it's easy to think that God is harsher than He really is. I also love President Nelson's talk 'Peacemakers Needed' and Moses 1:39, "For behold, this is my work and my glory--to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man."
