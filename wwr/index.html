Rivers

Aside from the rapids, there will be opportunities to jump in and feel the untamed might of the river carry you along in solitude. Pit your strength against nature's by trying to swim upriver!

Camping

Let the splashing river lull you to sleep for up to four nights in a row. Or hike up to a peak to pitch your tent, and let your concsiousness drift over a magnificent valley.

Rapids

The guides talk big, but there's nothing to fear from the 8 foot vertical rapids, cliff jumping, or 'riding the bull', as long as you're tough--and lucky!

More Than Just The Thrill

The river winds through a lush canyon surrounded by breathtaking vistas. Bathe in the majesty of soaring cliffs, knotted verdure, and curious wildlife. Dry Oar is about way more than just the rapids.
